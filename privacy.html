Privacy Policy.

How we handle the information you share with us.

Last updated: 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Stonehaven Lending (“Stonehaven,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information you provide through stonehavencre.com and when you enquire about financing. By using the site or submitting an enquiry, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry form, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, financing interest, and any details you share about your transaction. We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as your browser type and pages visited, to operate and improve the site.

How we use information

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry, evaluate and discuss financing, communicate with you about your request, and comply with legal and regulatory obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to provide the services you request — for example, with service providers who help us operate our business and process loan enquiries, and as required by law. Any such providers are expected to protect your information and use it only for the services they perform for us.

Data security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information you share. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to safeguard your information appropriately.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided by contacting us. You can also ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email office@stonehavencre.com or call +1 (800) 555-0100.
